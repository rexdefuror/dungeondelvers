The land of Kigia. Militaristic kingdom led by King Velubran Landram Ren and a group of sages called, Council of Grates. It is populated mostly by humans, but there are other species with the lands as well. Kings in the linage of Ren have ruled Kigia for more than thousand years, and while ruling with a firm totalitarian hand the kingdom has never refused to assist their neighbors in times of need, with the exception if they were in state of war. Nor have they ever allowed slavery to take root within it. The only thing they cared about was strength, if you are strong in body or mind you were treated better. Simple as that.

The principle of Strength as the locals called it was a common thing in Kigia, even things like judgment by combat existed and was offered to those who haven't committed any of the grave sins. Treason was considered the gravest sin of all, and that is exactly what led to the events of Great Rupture.

On the fifth day of Euros, fifth month of the year 3562, a horrifying event took place. Whole city of people was sacrificed in the attempt to summon a God into the world of Astana, and thus began the Age of Tempest.

Sages from the Council of Grates, betrayed their king and sacrificed their own people in order to further their power and influence in the world. However, they were themselves betrayed by the would-be God Thannanoth, the great demon general from another world, instead of summoning a God they summoned a power crystal from the demons home world that changed the spatial magic structure within Astana and started opening rift-like portals all around the world.
If one such rift remained open for more than several days, the rifts spatial structure would be complete and demons and monsters from various worlds would pour out from it, killing everything in their path.

But the world would balance itself even if nearly broken. From the darkness caused by the council, came a new age of heroes! Adventurers would travel to those rifts willingly and slay their Gatekeepers thus closing it permanently before the breach could occur. Many brave souls were lost while trying to discover more about these rifts.

A scroll was created, detailing the historical events that led to the Rupture, as well as the rules of the rifts discovered by adventurers of that time.


>**Birth of the Rupture**
>
>We were fooled and betrayed by those we trust most. Sages have nearly exterminated all races by summoning the red crystal. It disappeared as soon as it touched the land it was summoned in, and then the first rift opened. We did not know anything about them and it was through sheer force of will and courage that we prevailed. I, King Velubran leave this scroll as a reminder to our descendants. If... No, when the crystal regains its power someone will have to fight all over again. What we your predecessors can do, is at least reduce the sacrifices needed. We are truly sorry to leave you with this perilous task.
>
>**Rules**:
>
>Number of Rifts appear depending on how many souls are gathered in one place, large cities means more Rifts
>Rifts can appear even in secluded caves, they can however be located by Sorcerers with sensitivity to spatial magic
>Rifts will take one full week, ten days, to mature before the breach happens
>Monsters within the Rift cannot exit until the Rift breaks, however anyone can enter it
>Gatekeepers are the ones whose power fuels the Rift, they are bound to it with their life force
>Only by killing the Gatekeeper can the rift be closed
>Once the Gatekeeper is slain it the Rift will be closed within one hour
>There can be more than one Gatekeeper
>I wish you the best of luck!
>
>
>
>Forever in your debt,
>
>King Velubran Landram Ren
